access

From NRT

  • Narita Airport
  • Narita Express NEX
  • Shinjuku
  • Azusa limited express (あずさ)
  • Matsumoto

You can purchase train tickets of the entire journey to Matsumoto at the airport JR ticketing desk.

From HND

  • Haneda Airport
  • Airport Limousine Bus
  • Shinjuku
  • Azusa limited express (あずさ)
  • Matsumoto

You can purchase a ticket of Azusa limited express at the Shinjuku station.

From Shinjuku neighborhoods

  • Shinjuku
  • Azusa limited express (あずさ)
  • Matsumoto

From Tokyo station neighborhoods

  • Tokyo
  • Hokuriku Shinkansen (北陸新幹線)
  • Nagano
  • Shinano limited express (しなの)
  • Matsumoto

From Kansai area and Nagoya

  • Osaka, Kyoto etc.
  • Tokaido Shinkansen (東海道新幹線)
  • Nagoya
  • Shinano limited express (しなの)
  • Matsumoto

Note that most Tokaido Shinkansen cannot be covered by Japan Rail Pass.

Check https://visitmatsumoto.com/en/access/ for more access guide.
